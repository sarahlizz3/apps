Stremio Recommender

AI-powered personalized movie and TV show recommendations for Stremio

What It Is

Stremio Recommender is a custom Stremio addon that provides personalized movie and TV show recommendations based on your viewing preferences. It uses TMDb for metadata and Claude AI for intelligent scoring.

How It Works

  1. Taste Profile: You define your preferences (genres you like, themes you enjoy, actors you follow)
  2. Discovery: The addon fetches trending and popular titles from TMDb
  3. AI Scoring: Claude AI analyzes each title against your taste profile and scores it
  4. Recommendations: High-scoring titles appear in a dedicated catalog in Stremio

What You'll See in Stremio

Once configured, you'll have a new catalog called "AI Recommendations" that shows personalized picks. Titles are sorted by their match score, with the best matches at the top.

Tip

The addon runs locally on your machine. Your taste profile and viewing data never leave your computer.

Quick Start

If you've already set up the addon, here's the quick reference:

Common Tasks
