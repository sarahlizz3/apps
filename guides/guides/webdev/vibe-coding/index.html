What is Vibe Coding?

Vibe coding is a development approach where you describe what you want in natural language, and an AI assistant handles the implementation details. Instead of writing every line yourself, you guide the process at a higher level—focusing on outcomes rather than syntax.

Claude Code is Anthropic's command-line tool that brings this workflow directly to your terminal. It reads your codebase, understands context, proposes changes, and applies them with your approval.

How It Works

  1. You describe what you want

    Be as specific or general as you like. Focus on outcomes, not implementation.

  2. Claude analyzes your codebase

    It reads relevant files automatically to understand your project structure.

  3. Claude proposes changes

    Shows you diffs before applying anything—you see exactly what will change.

  4. You review and approve

    Accept, reject, or ask for modifications before any code is written.

  5. Claude applies the changes

    Files are modified in place once you approve.

Tip

Vibe coding isn't just about getting code written—it's also great for learning. Ask Claude to explain its choices, discuss tradeoffs, or explore alternatives.
